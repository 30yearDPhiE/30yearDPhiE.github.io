About The Reunion

Thank You!

A heartfelt thank you to everyone who joined us for our 30-year reunion on April 4th–6th, 2025! Your presence made the weekend truly unforgettable. From the laughter and hugs to the shared stories and new memories, we’re so grateful to have celebrated this milestone surrounded by the love and joy of our incredible sisterhood!

Your energy, kindness, and support helped make this event as special as the memories we’ve built over the last three decades. Thank you for making it such a magical weekend!
