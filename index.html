The Reunion

Join us April 4th-6th, 2025 for our 30-year reunion!

Come for an unforgettable celebration as we mark 30 years of sisterhood, shared memories, and lifelong friendships. This milestone reunion is the perfect chance to reconnect with old friends, reminisce about your unforgettable college days, and see how far we've all come since our time in DPhiE. Whether you’re hoping to catch up, share stories, or simply relive the good old days, this gathering will be full of laughter, warmth, and joy. Let's come together to honor the legacy we helped build, support new generations, and remember the bonds that have stayed with us through every stage of life. This is an amazing chance to reconnect, reflect, and create even more memories with your sisters!

What to Expect

Here’s what you can expect from our 30-year reunion weekend: the main event will be a formal evening of celebration, featuring inspiring speeches, a tasty dinner, and a night of dancing that’s sure to bring back some of our best memories. We’ll also honor our cherished traditions, making it a truly meaningful experience. But that’s just the beginning! Throughout the weekend, you’ll have opportunities to catch up and make new memories with a Bar Crawl through some of our favorite spots, have a Family Line Reunion, take a nostalgic Campus Tour, and enjoy a relaxed Brunch with delicious pastries. See our itinerary for more details.

This reunion will be an unforgettable weekend filled with laughter, love, and sisterhood. Don’t miss it!
