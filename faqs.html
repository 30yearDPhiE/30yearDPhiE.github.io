FAQs

What is the dress code?

Here's what to wear for each event:

  • Bar Crawl: Dress up in fun, festive attire of your choice! Just remember, no letters or DPhiE-related items.
  • Family Line Reunion: Business casual, springtime dressy, or brunch dressy — whatever feels polished and seasonally stylish!
  • RIT Tour: Wear whatever you're most comfortable in; we recommend comfortable shoes, as there will be lots of walking!
  • Formal Dinner: Black tie/formal attire, so dust off your finest gowns and tuxedos for a night of elegance!
  • Alumni Brunch: This is a time for chapter pride, so please wear letters, if possible!
  • Reunion Ceremony: Wear all black and your pins to honor our traditions and legacy.

What does my formal event ticket include?

Your ticket includes a buffet-style dinner with a delectable dessert, access to an open bar, and the joy of reconnecting with sisters. Each ticket admits one guest, so be sure to purchase an additional ticket for your date or any other guests you'd like to bring.

Can I bring my family to the reunion?

For those bringing family, here’s what to keep in mind: kids and families are welcome at the Family Line Reunion, Campus Tour, and Brunch, so feel free to bring any oved ones to these gatherings! However, due to the their nature, the Formal Dinner will be for guests 18+, and the Bar Crawl requires attendees to be 21+. Needless to say, our ceremony is a sister-only event, so no guests are permitted. We appreciate everyone’s understanding as we create an inclusive and enjoyable weekend for all!
