FAQs

What is the dress code?

Our Formal Dinner on Saturday evening calls for black tie/formal attire, so dust off your finest gowns and tuxedos for a night of elegance! For the rest of the weekend’s events, feel free to dress to the occasion and choose outfits that let you relax and enjoy. For our Reunion Ritual, please remember to wear all black to honor our traditions and legacy.

Can I bring my family to the reunion?

For those bringing family, here’s what to keep in mind: kids and families are welcome at the Family Line Reunion, Campus Tour, and Brunch, so feel free to bring any oved ones to these gatherings! However, due to the their nature, the Formal Dinner will be for guests 18+, and the Bar Crawl requires attendees to be 21+. Needless to say, our ritual is a sister-only event, so no guests are permitted. We appreciate everyone’s understanding as we create an inclusive and enjoyable weekend for all!
