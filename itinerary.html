Itinerary

Friday (4/4/2025)

chevron_right 8:00pm - 11:00pm

Bar Crawl (multiple locations)

Enjoy a night with all sisters 21+ on the town in our very own Downtown Rochester, featuring some of our favorite spots! As a note, all beverages are at your own cost directly to the venues.

Saturday (4/5/2025)

chevron_right 10:00am - 12:00pm

Family Line Reunion at the Lobozzo Alumni House

Get the family back together to reminisce in the memories of DPhiE across generations! Come ready to take some awesome pictures to see how your family has grown. We will start off as a whole group and then split off into different rooms for families to have privacy. Snacks and a Soda Bar will be provided!

chevron_right 12:30pm - 2:00pm

Optional Tour of RIT and DPhiE House

Have you seen how campus and our house have evolved over the last 10 years? Some campus trivia and adventures to come!

chevron_right 5:00pm - 10:00pm

Formal Dinner at La Luna

A formal evening of celebration, featuring inspiring speeches, a tasty dinner, and a night of dancing that’s sure to bring back some of our best memories. We’ll also honor our cherished traditions, making it a truly meaningful experience!

Sunday (4/6/2025)

chevron_right 10:00am - 12:00pm

Brunch at University Gallery

After a night of laughs and inspiration, join us for some self care, midmorning delights, and reminisce the celebrations of the weekend by adding to our scrapbook!

chevron_right 12:30pm - 2:00pm

Reunion Ceremony at the Glass Box Theater

Join us as we close out the weekend with a special event to commemorate our chapter's achievements and reflect on our progress over the years. As a gentle reminder, this event is sisters only and ritual attire is required (formal black business dress attire with pins).
